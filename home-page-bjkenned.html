About Me - Brett Kennedy

Hello all, my name is Brett Kennedy and I am a senior at Syracuse University on the Men's Lacrosse team. I grew up in Ridgewood, NJ and have been playing lacrosse ever since first grade. I graduated from Ridgewood High School in 2016 and have been at SU ever since!

  • Ridgewood Youth Lacrosse: 1st Grade - 5th Grade

  • Ridgewood Travel Lacrosse: 5th Grade - 8th Grade

  • Ridgewood Freshman Team: 2012 - 2013

  • Ridgewood Varsity Team: 2013 - 2016

  • Syracuse Men's Lacrosse: 2016 - Present
